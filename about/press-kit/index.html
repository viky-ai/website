The viky.ai platform, in a nutshell.

Pertimm is an innovative French company with more than 21 years of experience in artificial intelligence. Pertimm provides B2B semantic search solutions, with customers such as SoLocal, Leclerc, Cora, Showroomprivé, Clubmed, UCPA, Promod.

Back in 2017, while we were working on the search engine at Pertimm, we had the will to add some dialog into our search engine. Rather than dealing with a specific use case, we then wanted to address the more general subject of Natural Language Processing.

So we submitted our project to the Europeen Commission, within the Horizon 2020 program and got funded.

At the end of 2019, we launched the viky.ai SaaS platform. It's a technological component that allows user to collaboratively build and share bricks that analyse texts in any languages.

viky.ai allows technical teams to build engaging voice and text-based conversational interfaces, such as voice apps and chatbots. The platform also allows the integration of natural language comprehension within a search engine or the extraction of structured information from long texts.

As we love and believe in Open Source, we released viky.ai source code under MIT licence.
